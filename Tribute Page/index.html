Greg Mortenson

The man who provided education to hundreds of underprivileged communities

Here's a time line of Mr. Mortenson's life:

  • 1957 - Born in St. Cloud, Minnesota
  • 1975 - Served in the U.S. Military in Germany
  • 1977 - Awarded the Army Commendation Medal
  • 1983 - Graduated from the University of South Dakota
  • 1993 - Saves the life of Etienne Fine whilst trying to climb the world's second-highest mountain, K2, as a memorial to his sister, Christa
  • 1996 - Establishes Central Asia Institute (CAI)
  • 2006 - Raises over 4.6 million USD through fund-raisers and promotion of his book on Girls Education for CAI
  • 2007 - Co-authors of the New York Times bestselling book 'Three Cups of Tea'
  • 2010 - Awarded the Common Wealth Award for For Public Service

"If you want to thrive in Baltistan, you must respect our ways. The first time you share tea with a Balti, you are a stranger. The second time you take tea, you are an honored guest. The third time you share a cup of tea, you become family, and for our family, we are prepared to do anything, even die."

--Greg Mortenson
